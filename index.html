About VectorAlgoAI

A strategy research platform built to make trading logic testable, explainable, and harder to fool.

Company

VectorAlgoAI is being built as an AI quant research lab for independent traders. The platform focuses on strategy validation, risk realism, and decision transparency — helping traders move from intuition to structured research before deploying capital.

The long-term goal is not to become another signal provider. The goal is to build research infrastructure that shows why a strategy works, when it fails, and whether it deserves risk.
